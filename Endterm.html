About the Author

Welcome to my creative corner of the web! I'm Apple Joy Montebon, a passionate web designer with a keen interest in the captivating world of web animation. With a background in IT and a love for artistic expression, I specialize in crafting visually appealing and interactive online experiences through the power of animation. Over the course of my journey in web design and development, I've delved into the nuances of CSS animations, SVG animations, and Canvas animations. Each project serves as a canvas for me to blend technical expertise with a touch of creativity, bringing websites to life with smooth transitions, engaging effects, and user-friendly interfaces. Whether it's a subtle hover effect or a complex sequence of animated elements, I believe that web animation adds an extra layer of charm and interactivity to digital spaces. My commitment to delivering aesthetically pleasing and functional designs is evident in the showcase of CSS, SVG, and Canvas creations within this portfolio. As I continue to explore new horizons in the ever-evolving realm of web animation, I am excited about the prospect of transforming ideas into visually stunning realities. Join me on this journey, and let's make the web a more dynamic and enjoyable place for everyone.
